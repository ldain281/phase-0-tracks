Module 4.2: Release 1 - Even robots get cavities
- walk forward until counter is reached
- look down and rotate head 30 degrees left
- move left arm one foot left, lift hand up 6 inches, move hand forward 1 foot, open hand and grab white tube
- move left hand to front of body and rotate head to center and look at tube
- rotate left hand so small end of tube points to the right.
- move right hand to front of body and same height as tube
- with right hand fingers, grab small end of tube, rotate right hand counterclockwise, release and grab small end of tube, repeat 3 times and then pull to the right.
- lower right hand and open fingers
- move right arm one foot to right, rotate head to view 30 degrees to right, select and grab with right hand horizontal red and white plastic handle
- move right hand back to center in front of body
- with right had rotate white plastic handle parallel to body and vertically 0 degrees, pointing white bristles upward
- move left hand so pointed end of tube is 1/4 inch above bristles.
- at 1/4 speed move fingers together, depressing tube 1/8 inch
- lift right hand until hand height = mouth height
- rotate hand clockwise until bristles on rod are perpendicular to mouth and point to mouth
- open mouth, smile and move rod toward teeth until bristles touch teeth
